# QuestLearn Terminology Sheet

Use these exact terms across all Part I files.

## Actors

- `Student`
- `Instructor`
- `Academic Advisor`
- `Admin`

## Required Process Terms

- `email verification`
- `assignment management`
- `activity tracking`
- `course engagement analytics`
- `student performance analytics`
- `announcement`
- `advisor-student assignment`
- `automated feedback`
- `recommended next steps`

## Naming Rules

- Do not alternate between `Advisor` and `Academic Advisor` in final submission text.
- Do not merge `Register` and `Login` into one label unless the diagram intentionally shows a combined high-level use case.
- Use `assignment` explicitly wherever TT7L requires it. Do not hide it under only `assessment`.
- Use `announcement` for admin communication items and `notification` for delivery events.
- Use the same entity names in the ERD and the use-case narrative.
